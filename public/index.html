The Busy Guardian

Experience based matchmaking for Destiny 2

Get Started

Play endgame activities without wasting time looking for a fireteam. We will match you with a balanced team ahead of time, so your game time is focused on playing the game.

Configure a profile with the platform(s) and time(s) you play. Add the activities you're interested in, and the experience you have playing them.

Every day at reset (9 AM PST) we will try to match you with other players to create a fireteam with a mix of experiences, and plenty of time to complete an activity.

Whenever you're matched we'll notify you via email and/or Discord. Confirm you can make it to hold your spot. We'll also send you a calendar invite so you don't forget.
